Policies

What you can expect

Liability & DOA Parts

I am not responsible for parts that arrive dead-on-arrival (DOA). If a component fails before or during assembly, I'll diagnose the issue and assist with the RMA process — but warranty claims go through the manufacturer directly. I handle the build; the parts are your responsibility to return if needed.

Post-Delivery Defects

All builds are tested before delivery. If something fails within 7 days of delivery due to assembly error on my part, I'll fix it at no charge. Hardware failures after delivery are covered under individual part warranties — not my labor fee.

Turnaround Time

Standard turnaround is 1 day from the time all parts are confirmed and in my hands. I will always communicate any delays.

Payment

Payment is due upon part orders. Accepted: Cashapp, Zelle, cash, Venmo, or PayPal.

Cancellations

Cancellations are accepted before parts are ordered with no charge. Once parts have been sourced, any non-returnable items are the customer's responsibility. I will always work with you to find a fair resolution.

Service Area

CadeBuilds currently serves the Carmel, Indiana area and surrounding communities. Delivery and setup add-on is available within a reasonable local radius. Contact me to confirm your area is covered before submitting a request.
